# Group - InfraService : HOWTO - Group Membership Management Tool
Created by Shah, Rupen on Feb 21, 2019
# Group Membership Management Tool **Synopsis**: This is a tool that owners of an Active Directory group, can use to manage (add/remove) membership of those groups. The groups could be 2FA groups for access to respective Azure applications or any other AD group that is used to authorize user access to specific file share or access within an application. To claim ownership of a group, you can open a Jira ticket under Infra project, Windows component. Please make sure you provide justification supporting your claim to the group ownership. **Instructions**: * Open a browser and launch [https://groupid.nyt.net](https://groupid.nyt.net) ![](attachments/78880577/78880576.png) * Login with your ENT credentials to get you to this page: ![](attachments/78880577/78880578.png) * On the left Navigation pane, click on My SGs, to see all the groups that you have permissions to manage ![](attachments/78880577/78880579.png) * Click on the group you want to manage membership or add Owners ![](attachments/78880577/78880580.png) * To add members, Go to the Members Tab. Enter the user's EIN in the 'Members to Add' space and click on the green check mark ![](attachments/78880577/78880581.png) on the right ![](attachments/78880577/78880582.png) * This should resolve the EIN to the user's name. Click OK, and Submit Pap * To remove a member, open up the group once again, and enter the EIN number in the 'Members to Remove' field, Click the green arrow to resolve the user name, click OK and Submit. * To add additional Owners for the group, Open up the Group and go to the Owners tab ![](attachments/78880577/78880583.png) * Click on the green check box ![](attachments/78880577/78880581.png) next to the 'Owner' field to enable editing ![](attachments/78880577/78880584.png) * Type in the EIN of the user you want to add as an Owner and resolve the username by clicking on the green check box ![](attachments/78880577/78880581.png) again. * Click OK, and the Submit ![](attachments/78880577/78880585.png) * You may close the browser to exit the utility.
